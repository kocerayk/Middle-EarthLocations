The Locations

Welcome to Middle-earth

Embark on an epic journey through the rich and enchanting world of Middle-earth. Discover its landscapes, history, and the tales of its great heroes.

The Shire

The Shire, a peaceful and idyllic region, is the homeland of the Hobbits. Known for its lush green fields and rolling hills, it is a place of simplicity and contentment.

Rivendell

Rivendell, the Last Homely House East of the Sea, is an Elven sanctuary hidden in the foothills of the Misty Mountains. It is renowned for its beauty and tranquility.

Helm's Deep

Helm's Deep, a stronghold in the White Mountains, is known for its defensive prowess during the War of the Ring. It played a crucial role in the defense against Saruman's forces.

Gondor

Gondor, the great city of men, is a land of towering fortresses and rich history. Its capital, Minas Tirith, stands as a beacon of hope and strength against the forces of darkness.

Mordor

Mordor, the dark land of Sauron, is a realm of desolation and evil. The ominous Mount Doom looms over the barren landscape, a symbol of the dark lord's power.
